How a session runs

  1. 01

    Choose a length

    25 for a quick sprint, 50 for something that needs momentum. The ring resizes to match.

  2. 02

    Work until it ends

    The ring empties as time passes. No pop-ups, no progress bar begging for attention.

  3. 03

    Take the break

    When the timer hits zero, Focal switches to a short rest countdown automatically.
